Vrinda Store Sales Analysis 2022
📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the 2022 sales data of the Vrinda Store, a fictional e-commerce entity. The goal is to uncover actionable insights regarding customer behavior, regional performance, and channel effectiveness to drive a projected 15–20% revenue growth in 2023 (p. 2).
🎯 Business Objectives
The primary objectives of this analysis were to:
Identify top-performing states and underutilized regions (p. 3).
Analyze gender and age-based purchasing behavior (p. 3).
Evaluate the effectiveness of different sales channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.) (p. 3).
Recommend data-driven strategies for inventory and marketing (p. 3).
🛠️ Tools Used
Microsoft Excel: Used for data cleaning, processing, and analysis using Pivot Tables and Formulas (p. 5).
Microsoft PowerPoint: Used to create the final insights presentation (p. 1).
📊 Data Methodology
Data Sourcing: Utilized a sample retail dataset from data analysis educator Rishabh Mishra (p. 5).
Data Cleaning:
Removed unnecessary columns (p. 5).
Standardized gender values (p. 5).
Formatted date columns for time-series analysis (p. 5).
Analysis Techniques: Applied trend analysis, segmentation, and pivot filtering to derive insights (p. 5).
📈 Key Findings
Customer Segment: Women drive the majority of sales, accounting for 64% of total revenue (p. 6).
Top Regions: Maharashtra (2.99M) and Karnataka (2.65M) are the leading states, contributing nearly 5.6M INR in sales (p. 7).
Primary Channel: Amazon is the dominant sales platform, representing 35% of all orders (p. 8).
Operational Health: The store maintains a high 92% fulfillment rate, indicating efficient logistics (p. 9).
💡 Recommendations
Targeted Marketing: Prioritize women-centric product lines and loyalty programs (p. 11).
Inventory Management: Increase stock levels in high-performing states like Maharashtra and Karnataka (p. 11).
Channel Strategy: Optimize Amazon promotions while diversifying presence on Myntra and Flipkart (p. 11).
Seasonality: Boost promotions during March and festive months to align with peak demand (p. 2).
🚀 Roadmap for 2023
Short-term (0-3 months): Launch female-focused campaigns and expand regional inventory (p. 12).
Medium-term (3-6 months): Pilot expansion in new states and optimize platform-specific promotions (p. 12).
Long-term (6+ months): Strengthen the logistics network to further reduce cancellations (p. 12).
