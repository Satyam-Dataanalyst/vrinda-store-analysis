# 📊 Vrinda Store Sales Analysis 2022

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the 2022 sales data of the **Vrinda Store**, a fictional e-commerce entity. The goal is to uncover actionable insights regarding customer behavior, regional performance, and channel effectiveness to drive a projected **15–20% revenue growth** in 2023.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
The primary objectives of this analysis were to:
*   **Identify** top-performing states and underutilized regions.
*   **Analyze** gender and age-based purchasing behavior.
*   **Evaluate** the effectiveness of different sales channels like Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
*   **Recommend** data-driven strategies for inventory and marketing.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** Used for data cleaning, processing, and analysis using Pivot Tables and Formulas.
* **Microsoft PowerPoint:** Used to create the final insights presentation.

---

## 📊 Data Methodology
* **Data Sourcing:** Utilized a sample retail dataset from data analysis educator Rishabh Mishra.
* **Data Cleaning:**
  * Removed unnecessary columns.
  * Standardized gender values.
  * Formatted date columns for time-series analysis.
* **Analysis Techniques:** Applied trend analysis, segmentation, and pivot filtering to derive insights.

---

## 📈 Key Findings
* **Customer Segment:** Women drive the majority of sales, accounting for 64% of total revenue.
* **Top Regions:** Maharashtra (2.99M) and Karnataka (2.65M) are the leading states, contributing nearly 5.6M INR in sales.
* **Primary Channel:** Amazon is the dominant sales platform, representing 35% of all orders.
* **Operational Health:** The store maintains a high 92% fulfillment rate, indicating efficient logistics.

---

## 💡 Recommendations
* **Targeted Marketing:** Prioritize women-centric product lines and loyalty programs.
* **Inventory Management:** Increase stock levels in high-performing states like Maharashtra and Karnataka.
* **Channel Strategy:** Optimize Amazon promotions while diversifying presence on Myntra and Flipkart.
* **Seasonality:** Boost promotions during March and festive months to align with peak demand.

---

## 🚀 Roadmap for 2023
* **Short-term (0-3 months):** Launch female-focused campaigns and expand regional inventory.
* **Medium-term (3-6 months):** Pilot expansion in new states and optimize platform-specific promotions.
* **Long-term (6+ months):** Strengthen the logistics network to further reduce cancellations.
